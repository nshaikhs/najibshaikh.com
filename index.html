How I Think

Operating philosophy

01

I build from zero, not optimize from ten.

My best work happens when there's no playbook. No existing team. No proven GTM. Just a problem and a mandate to figure it out.

02

Bias toward action, but not blind action.

I move fast, but in the right direction. That means understanding the real problem before building anything.

03

The CEO should spend 80% of their time on product.

My job is to own everything that isn't the founder's superpower — so they can focus on what moves the company.

04

I don't build process for process's sake.

Every system I build has one test: does the team move faster with it than without it? If not, kill it.
