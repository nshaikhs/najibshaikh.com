About

Pakistan to New York, via economics, startups, and a lot of building.

I was born in Lahore, Pakistan and spent my childhood between Lahore and the UAE, attending the International School of Choueifat. The kind of upbringing where you learn early that the world is bigger than any one place — and that you have to figure things out wherever you land.

I studied Economics and Politics at LUMS (Lahore University of Management Sciences) — one of the top universities in South Asia — and graduated with Distinguished Honors. Then I moved to New York in 2020, right as everything shut down. Perfect timing to join a fast-growing telecom startup.

Today I'm based in the NYC area. Married to Elena. First baby on the way. That last part still doesn't feel entirely real.
