How I Think

Operating philosophy

01

I build from zero and scale to a hundred.

I join when there's no playbook, no team, no proven GTM. I create the infrastructure, then I scale it. $20M to $150M. $80M to $230M. The building and the scaling are the same job.

02

Move fast, but know where you're going.

Speed without direction is just chaos. I understand the real problem before building anything, then execute without hesitation.

03

I don't build process for process's sake.

Every system I build has one test: does the team move faster with it than without it? If not, kill it.
